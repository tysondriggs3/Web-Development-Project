Education Beyone Schooling

Tom Brady's education extends well beyond high school and college school work. Tom spent 23 seasons in the National Football League playing the quarterback position. The quarterback position is known to be one of the most study intensive positions in the NFL. During his 23 seasons as a quarterback, he had to learn and memorize the game of football. Tom knows the game better than almost anyone. Much of Tom's success in the National Football League can be attributed to his knowledge of how the game works.

Along with Tom's education in football, he is also very knowledgable when it comes to nutrition. He is known for how well he has taken care of his body in order to play for as long as he did. Tom Brady, along with his friend and trainer Alex Guerrero, founded TB12, a sports and wellness company. Within this company, Tom gives advice on nutrition, workouts, and how to take care of the body. He has even published his own book called "The TB12 Method."
